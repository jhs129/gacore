How can I lower my cancer risk?

Limit alcohol use

Drinking alcohol, whether beer, wine, or liquor, increases your risk of breast cancer. It's recommended to limit alcohol to one drink per day.

Maintain a healthy weight

Being overweight raises your breast cancer risk. Aim for a BMI between 18.5 and 25 to maintain a healthy weight.
